Luhuagames is a mobile game developer and we do not save your personal data in our server. According to Facebook policy, we have to provide User Data Deletion Callback URL or Data Deletion Instructions URL.




How to delete your activities for Luhuagames



Step 1: Go to your Facebook Account’s Setting & Privacy. Click “Settings”



Step 2: Look for “Apps and Websites” and you will see all of the apps and websites you linked with your Facebook.



Step 3: Search and Click “Luhuagames” in the search bar.



Step 4: Scroll and click “Remove”.



Congratulations, you have succesfully removed your app activities.
